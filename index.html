About InAmigos Foundation

Founded in 2020, InAmigos Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to creating positive and sustainable change in society. Headquartered in Chhattisgarh, the foundation works across multiple sectors including education, community welfare, environmental conservation, women empowerment, animal welfare, and skill development.

With a strong network of volunteers, interns, and social impact professionals, the organization strives to address real-world challenges and improve the quality of life for underserved communities. Through transparency, accountability, and community participation, InAmigos Foundation continues to expand its reach and impact across India.

The foundation runs several impactful initiatives such as Project Seva, Project Bachpanshala, Project Jeev, Project Udaan, Project Prakriti, and Project Vikas. These programs focus on supporting vulnerable communities, promoting education, protecting animals, empowering women, encouraging environmental responsibility, and enhancing employability through skill development opportunities.

Over the years, the organization has supported thousands of beneficiaries, planted thousands of saplings, provided assistance to communities in need, and trained a large number of interns through practical learning experiences. Every initiative reflects the foundation’s commitment to building a more inclusive, compassionate, and empowered society.

At InAmigos Foundation, we believe that meaningful change happens when individuals come together with a shared purpose. By volunteering, collaborating, or supporting our initiatives, everyone has the opportunity to contribute to a better future for communities across India.
